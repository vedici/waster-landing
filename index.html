WASTER

A game that can change your mindset

Created By Vedici Studio

Play Now !

Control your player

Hold your finger to move player to your specified direction. Hold Left side of the screen to move left, and Hold Right side of the screen to move Right

Avoid Obstacle

If you want to survive, you have to avoid the obstacles that comes along the way. once you hit the obstacles, you lose!

Pick the correct waste to gain additional point

you can pick a waste to gain additional point, but be careful because you dont want to pick the wrong type of waste ! it can reduce your points

Choose the right condition!

you can change your player condition to adjust with the waste that you want to pick. change the state of the player by pushing the change button that located on the bottom of the screen below the player. Player have 2 condition, first one is player who can pick organic waste, this condition is represented by green garbage truck, and the second one is player who can pick an-organic waste, this condition is represented by yellow garbage truck.
